# Project Vision

## DischargeWise

**An AI-Assisted Clinical Decision Support Platform for Safe Hospital Discharge**

---

## Vision

To support clinicians in making safer and more informed discharge decisions through transparent, trustworthy, and human-centered artificial intelligence.

---

## Mission

DischargeWise is an AI-assisted clinical decision support platform designed to support hospital discharge planning by assessing a patient's risk of 30-day readmission before discharge. Rather than functioning as a standalone prediction tool, the platform combines machine learning, explainable AI, and clinically meaningful workflow guidance to help clinicians better understand patient risk and make informed decisions within their existing clinical practice.

The system is designed to assist healthcare professionals—not replace them. Final clinical decisions always remain the responsibility of the treating physician.

---

## Why This Matters

Hospital discharge is one of the most critical transition points in patient care. Decisions are often made under significant time pressure while relying on large volumes of heterogeneous clinical information. Although hospitals routinely collect extensive patient data, transforming that data into clear, actionable insight remains a significant challenge.

DischargeWise addresses this gap by converting complex clinical data into transparent risk assessments that clinicians can quickly understand, evaluate, and incorporate into the discharge planning process.

---

## Target Users

### Primary Users

* Physicians responsible for discharge decisions
* Discharge planning clinicians

### Secondary Users

* Discharge nurses
* Care coordinators

---

## Core Design Principles

Every design decision within DischargeWise follows five fundamental principles:

1. **AI assists clinical judgment—it never replaces it.**
2. **Every prediction should be understandable and explainable.**
3. **The interface should minimize cognitive load and support rapid decision-making.**
4. **Patient safety takes priority over predictive performance alone.**
5. **Trust, transparency, and usability are essential requirements—not optional features.**

---

## Success Criteria

DischargeWise will be considered successful when:

* Clinicians can quickly understand both the predicted risk and the reasoning behind it.
* The platform integrates naturally into existing discharge planning workflows.
* Predictions are presented with clear explanations that support clinical confidence.
* The system enables more informed decision-making while preserving full physician responsibility.
* The platform demonstrates that explainable and human-centered AI can provide practical value in real clinical environments.

---

## Design Philosophy

Technology should adapt to clinical practice—not require clinicians to adapt to technology.

DischargeWise is designed around the needs of clinicians rather than around machine learning models. Predictive algorithms are only one component of the system; the primary objective is to deliver trustworthy clinical decision support that improves usability, transparency, and ultimately patient care.
