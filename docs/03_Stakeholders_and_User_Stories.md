# Stakeholders and User Stories

**Project:** DischargeWise

**Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Approved

---

# Purpose

This document identifies the key stakeholders of the DischargeWise platform and captures their needs through user stories. These user stories serve as the foundation for defining the system's functional requirements.

---

# Stakeholders

## 1. Physician

### Role
Makes the final decision regarding patient discharge.

### Goals

- Reduce preventable hospital readmissions.
- Make safe and evidence-informed discharge decisions.
- Quickly identify patients requiring additional clinical review.
- Maintain full responsibility for the final discharge decision.

### Challenges

- Limited time for reviewing patient information.
- Large volumes of heterogeneous clinical data.
- Difficulty identifying interacting risk factors.
- Cognitive overload during busy clinical shifts.

### Needs

- A clear readmission risk assessment.
- Explainable AI predictions.
- The most influential risk factors.
- Workflow-oriented review suggestions.
- A fast and intuitive interface.

---

## 2. Nurse

### Role

Supports discharge preparation and ensures discharge procedures are completed.

### Goals

- Ensure patients are ready for discharge.
- Verify that discharge protocols have been completed.
- Improve communication with physicians.

### Challenges

- Limited visibility into overall patient risk.
- Time pressure during discharge preparation.
- Coordinating activities across the clinical team.

### Needs

- A simple patient risk overview.
- Clear discharge readiness information.
- Easy access to relevant patient information.

---

## 3. Patient

### Role

Receives the discharge decision and follows post-discharge instructions.

### Goals

- Leave the hospital safely.
- Reduce the likelihood of unexpected readmission.
- Feel confident that discharge decisions are well supported.

### Needs

- Confidence in the discharge process.
- Clear follow-up instructions.
- Safe continuity of care.

---

## 4. Hospital Administrator

### Role

Monitors healthcare quality and organizational performance.

### Goals

- Reduce hospital readmission rates.
- Improve quality of care.
- Optimize healthcare resources.
- Support evidence-based clinical practice.

### Needs

- Reliable performance indicators.
- Readmission statistics and trends.
- Trustworthy AI-assisted decision support.

---

# User Stories

## Physician

### US-1

**As a physician, I want to view a patient's predicted readmission risk so that I can make safer discharge decisions.**

### US-2

**As a physician, I want to understand why a patient is classified as high risk so that I can validate the prediction before making a decision.**

### US-3

**As a physician, I want to receive workflow-oriented review suggestions so that I do not overlook important discharge considerations.**

### US-4

**As a physician, I want to access detailed clinical information only when needed so that the interface remains simple and efficient.**

---

## Nurse

### US-5

**As a nurse, I want to review the patient's discharge readiness so that I can complete all required discharge procedures.**

### US-6

**As a nurse, I want to quickly identify patients requiring additional attention so that I can better coordinate discharge activities.**

---

## Patient

### US-7

**As a patient, I want confidence that my discharge decision has been carefully reviewed so that I feel safe returning home.**

---

## Hospital Administrator

### US-8

**As a hospital administrator, I want to monitor readmission trends so that I can evaluate quality improvement initiatives.**
